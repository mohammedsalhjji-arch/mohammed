About CSBI

Advancing sustainability through innovation, research, and collaboration.

Who We Are

The Centre for Sustainable Business and Innovation (CSBI) is a multidisciplinary hub committed to advancing sustainability through innovation-led business transformation. CSBI connects academic knowledge with real-world application to create measurable impact.

Our Purpose

CSBI supports Saudi Vision 2030 by contributing to economic diversification, environmental sustainability, private sector growth, and knowledge-based development.

Our Approach

CSBI adopts a systems-thinking approach to address interconnected challenges such as energy transition, sustainable urban development, digital transformation, and inclusive economic growth.

Core Activities

Research

Interdisciplinary research that supports policy, business strategy, and innovation.

Innovation

Developing and piloting scalable solutions for real-world sustainability challenges.

Engagement

Collaborating with industry, government, and civil society to create impact.

Education

Building capacity through training, executive education, and knowledge sharing.
