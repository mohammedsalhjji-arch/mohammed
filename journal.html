ESBR Journal

About the Journal

Environment, Society and Business Review (ESBR) is a double-blind, peer-reviewed, open-access multidisciplinary journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across a broad spectrum of academic disciplines.

Focus and Scope

  • Sustainable Business Models and Strategies
  • Environmental Policy and Corporate Compliance
  • Climate Change and Business Adaptation
  • Technology Innovation and Management
  • Green Finance and Sustainable Investment
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Open Access Policy

ESBR provides immediate open access to its content to support greater global exchange of knowledge.

Publication Frequency

Two issues are published every year: January and June.
